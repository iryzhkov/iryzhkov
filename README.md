# Hi, I'm Igor

Machine Learning Engineer at Meta working on ads auction optimization. Previously built LLM infrastructure at SliceX AI, researched efficient attention mechanisms at Microsoft, and shipped front-end features at Google and Amazon.

M.S. in Artificial Intelligence from Northwestern University.

## What I'm into

**ML & AI** -- by day I work on large-scale ML models for ad systems. Outside of work I stay hands-on with deep learning fundamentals, from implementing transformers from scratch to experimenting with local LLM deployments on my homelab.

**Self-hosting** -- I run a Proxmox-based homelab with reverse proxies, VPN mesh networking, containerized services, and local AI model serving. At big companies, most infrastructure is abstracted away -- running my own forces me to actually understand networking, DNS, TLS, storage, and deployment at a level you don't get when everything is a managed service. It makes me a better engineer at work.

**Track days & data** -- I drive a Subaru BRZ on track and combine my engineering mindset with motorsports. I built [86 Challenge](https://86.ryzhkov.dev) -- a telemetry web app for the GR86/BRZ/FRS time trial community where drivers can upload RaceChrono data, compare laps, and track progress. I also write about my track experiences and car modifications on my [blog](https://blog.ryzhkov.dev).
